Logging into the MedSavant Server

In order to connect to a MedSavant server, you will need:

  • your username
  • your password
  • the server hostname
  • the server port
  • the database name

You can get these from your administrator.

Logging In

At the MedSavant login screen, enter your username and password. Check that your connection settings are filled out properly by pressing the Connection Settings button. The click Login.

You'll be taken to the Summary section upon logging in. This and other sections are explained briefly in the next part.
