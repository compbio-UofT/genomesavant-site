Managing Patients and Cohorts

After logging into MedSavant, or clicking the 'Project' section, a summary will be displayed for the current project indicating the number of individuals and cohorts. Additional patients (individuals) and cohorts can be added via the 'Patients' and Cohorts subsections, respectively, from this section.

Adding and Removing Patients

Patients can be added manually, one at a time by clicking the icon near the bottom left of the MedSavant window. This pops up the "Add Patient" window, containing various fields, all of which are optional except the Hospital ID, that can be filled out. Some fields - including DNA ID(s), BAM URL(s), and Phenotypes support multiple values, which should be separated by new lines. BAM URL(s) should be prefixed with the appropriate protocol -- e.g. http:// or https://. After adding an individual, the hospital ID for that individual will be listed in the left pane, and selecting that identifier will display the record for that individual. Individuals may also be removed by clicking the icon located near the bottom left of the MedSavant window.

Selecting an individual in in the left pane will load the corresponding record in the right pane. In addition to basic patient information, the pedigree for the patient can be viewed by clicking the 'Show' button beside 'Pedigree'. Pedigrees are generated based on the Family ID, Hospital ID, and IDs of the mother and father. Specifically, a node in the pedigree graph is created for each individual that has the same the Family ID of the selected patient, and an edge links two family members whenever the hospital ID of the parent matches the "ID of Mother" or "ID of Father" field associated with the child (). Two buttons in the upper right of the pedigree graph allow the font size to be increased () or decreased ().

Importing and Exporting Patients

Patients may also be exported or imported to or from a file of commas separated values (.csv file), which can be read and written from most database and spreadsheet programs, including Excel. To export a file, click the button near the bottom left of the MedSavant window, after which you will be prompted for a filename. which are typically exported by a spreadsheet program (e.g. Excel) or database program. The CSV file For importing, a .csv file may contain records for multiple patients, one per row, and must contain at a minimum the following columns:

Column TitleRequired?Description
family_idNThe family identifier. Only individuals matching this identifier will appear in the pedigree.
hospital_idYThe Hospital identifier. Individuals with the same family ID as this patient, and that refer to this hospital ID as a father or mother will appear as children in the pedigree graph.
idbiomomNIdentifier of patient's mother.
idbiodadNIdentifier of patient's father.
genderNThe gender of the patient, specified as an integer where 0: unspecified, 1: Male, 2: Female.
affectedNSet to "true" if this patient is affected by ???, or "false" otherwise
dna_idsNA semicolon-delimited list of DNA Identifiers for the sequences associated with this patient.
bam_urlNA semicolon-delimited list of URLs to the BAM files associated with this patient. (Click here for .bam format specifications)
phenotypesNA semicolon-delimited list of phenotypes observed in this individual.

Most fields are optional, and if information is not available they can be left blank. If one of the optional fields is always missing -- for example, you may not know the BAM urls for any of the patients -- the column will still be required, though each entry will be blank. Also, in addition to the above fields, your administrator may have defined other fields to be associated with patients, and these fields must be defined in the .csv also. Adding Patients to a Cohort

Patients can be added to a Cohort by selecting one or more individuals within the "Patients" subsection, and then clicking "Assign individual(s) to Cohort" (). The Cohorts themselves are created in a separate "Cohorts" subsection, within the "Project" section. In this subsection, you can add and delete Cohorts by clicking the or icons, respectively, both located towards the lower left of the MedSavant window (). Clicking any Cohort in the left pane will display the members of that Cohort in the right pane. To remove an individual from a Cohort, click the icon immediately below the list of users.
